Privacy Policy

If you provide your "bit.ly" API key it will only be used for purposes of bookmarking places using your "bit.ly" account. It will not be stored permanently by "my wahi", however, it may be temporarily stored in a browser cookie (e.g. so that you do not need to provide it every time you bookmark a place).
