About my wahi

Andy Simonds

"Wahi" is the Hawaiian (and Maori!) word for "place." This site, my wahi, lets you bookmark your favorite places and discover those other people have already bookmarked. While my wahi requires no registration, you can connect your bit.ly account, if you have one, to keep track of places you personally add.

Data generated through my wahi are hereby released in the public domain, so please keep that in mind as you use the site. Enjoy.
